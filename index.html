Recipe rating Prediction

Can the ingredients and instructions tell us anything??

In this project, we want to see if it is possible to accurately predict the rating for a given recipe just by looking at its ingredients and instructions alone. This does seem to be a hard task. If you think about it, just knowing the ingredients or just knowing the instructions can't tell you much about how the dish is going to taste like. And taste is what utimately decides the ratings. Still, we want to give a shot at it. Who knows, may be we might get some interesting results.
